Message without filling your contact list

Direct Message is a convenient tool for anyone who wants to start a WhatsApp chat without saving the number first. Enter the phone number, tap start chat, and the app opens the conversation in WhatsApp instantly. It is quick, simple, and hassle-free.

To make things even easier, the app supports copy and paste. Copy a phone number from anywhere — a website, email, notes, or another app — and paste it directly into Direct Message to start the chat immediately.

If the phone number is not on WhatsApp, WhatsApp will notify you automatically.

Many of us need to message someone only once — a delivery driver, technician, cab driver, customer, or other temporary contact. Saving those numbers permanently clutters your contact list. Sometimes those contacts can also see your WhatsApp status and profile, which you may not want.

Direct Message helps you avoid that by letting you start chats instantly without saving numbers. Keep your contact list clean, protect your privacy, and send messages more efficiently.

Send direct messages easily, quickly, and without cluttering your contacts with Direct Message. The app is designed for both personal and business communication, making quick messaging easier and more efficient.

Why use Direct Message?

Many people work with new phone numbers every day — deliveries, customer support, logistics, online orders, and business communication. Saving every number permanently is not practical and makes contact lists hard to manage.

Direct Message lets you start conversations when you need them, save time, and keep your phonebook clean. It is a useful tool for business owners, sales teams, drivers, logistics companies, freelancers, and online sellers who frequently message new contacts.

How to use

Five quick steps from number to conversation.

  1. Enter or paste a phone number, or use automatic number detection.
  2. Select the country code.
  3. Type a message or choose a quick message.
  4. Choose the messaging platform or SMS.
  5. The chat opens instantly.

Privacy advantage

Because temporary numbers are not saved in your contacts, your contact list stays organised and more private.
