Why Direct Message

Fast, simple direct messaging — say what you need without turning every number into a permanent contact.

Chat without saving contacts

Start a conversation using a phone number you have not stored on your device — ideal for one-off or infrequent chats.

Optionally prepare text before the chat opens so you can send the right wording in one step.

🔗

Shareable direct links

Generate a link others can use to open the same style of direct chat — handy when you want a frictionless handoff.

🌙

Dark mode

Comfortable in low light with an interface that supports dark theme for easier reading day or night.

🚀

No account required to start

Focus on getting the message out — choose a flow that does not depend on signing up before you can chat.

Fast and light on battery

Tuned for quick delivery and efficient use of data and power so you can rely on it throughout the day.
